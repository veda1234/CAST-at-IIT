Other water quality toolboxes

The links provides tools that can be used for computing different aspects related with the quality of water. These tools are developed independently by our partners and colleagues. The tools are:

  1. Surface water quality index and models (e.g. DO-Sag)
  2. Groundwater models (Prof. Al Valocchi, Uni-Illinois-UC)
  3. Aquifer-Storage-Recharge (ASR) models (Prof. Charles Werth, Uni-Texas, Austin)

These above toolboxes complement the toolboxes provided in this site.
